About LLTB Electrical

LLTB Electrical is based in Burntwood and proudly serves homeowners throughout Staffordshire and the West Midlands. We specialise in modern electrical upgrades, helping customers replace outdated accessories with stylish, practical and energy-efficient alternatives. From socket replacements and switch upgrades to modern lighting, video doorbells and smart home improvements, we focus on delivering reliable workmanship, attention to detail and great customer service. Whether you're upgrading a single room or refreshing your entire property, we're here to help.
